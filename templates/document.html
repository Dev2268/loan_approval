Loan Application Documents

Proof of Identity (Any one of the following):

  • Aadhar Card
  • Passport
  • Voter ID card
  • Driving License
  • PAN Card

Proof of Residence/Address (Any one of the following):

  • Aadhar Card
  • Passport
  • Voter ID card
  • Driving License
  • Recent utility bills (Electricity bill, Water bill, Gas bill)
  • Rental agreement

Income Proof (Depending on the type of loan and borrower's occupation):

  • Salary slips for salaried individuals
  • Income Tax Returns (ITR) and Balance Sheet for self-employed individuals
  • Form 16
  • Bank statements

Employment Proof (For salaried individuals):

  • Employment verification letter from the employer
  • Offer letter from the employer

Business Proof (For self-employed individuals):

  • Business registration documents
  • Business address proof

Property Documents (For Property-Related Loans):

  • Title deeds
  • Sale agreement
  • Property tax receipts
  • Approved building plan

Guarantor Documents (If applicable):

  • Identity and address proofs of the guarantor
  • Income proof of the guarantor

Educational Documents (For Educational Loans):

  • Admission letter from the educational institution
  • Fee structure and expenses details
  • Academic records and certificates
